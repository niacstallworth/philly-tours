Delete Account

Request deletion of your account and associated backend data.

How To Request Deletion

Email info@foundersthreads.org with the subject line Philly Tours account deletion request.

Include the email address connected to your Philly Tours profile so we can locate the correct records.

What Gets Deleted

When requested, Philly Tours can delete account-associated backend records such as membership, purchase, newsletter, support, and deletion-request records tied to your account.

What You Can Delete In The App

The app also lets you delete the local profile stored on your device. That clears on-device profile and local progress data, but it does not automatically remove backend records tied to purchases or support requests.

Timing

Deletion requests are reviewed and fulfilled manually. If we need more information to verify the request, we may reply by email before completing deletion.

Need Help?

For support with the deletion process, contact info@foundersthreads.org.
