The route pages are becoming more useful before the walk even starts.

We are shaping them into a place where visitors can understand the arc of a route, preview key stops, and decide whether they want a self-guided experience or a live guide.

## Recent improvements

- Better route context and story framing
- Clearer stop order and map previews
- More visible guided-tour purchase moments

## What comes next

We want each route page to feel like a compact field guide: part editorial story, part planning surface, and part launch point into the mobile experience.

That means more updates around blog content, route curation notes, and founder commentary directly inside the webapp.
