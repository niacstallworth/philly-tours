We just turned on **in-person tour guide checkout** in the Philly Tours webapp.

This makes it easier for schools, visitors, and community groups to turn a self-guided route into a hosted walk with a live guide, coordinated pacing, and the matching map pack for the group.

## What is included

- A guided version of the selected route
- Coordinated pacing for the group
- The matching Google map pack for navigation and handoff

## Why this matters

The webapp has always been strong for planning, previewing stops, and exploring the story structure of a route. Guided checkout closes the loop by making it simple to move from browsing into a real hosted tour.

We are also improving the internal notification flow so Founders Threads gets alerted as soon as one of these bookings is purchased.
