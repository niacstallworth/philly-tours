# Store Screenshot Captions

Use these captions when turning the raw device captures into App Store, Play Store, website, or social launch graphics.

Submission decision: use raw `core-*` screenshots for App Store and Play Store uploads. Use framed screenshots for marketing, social, website, README, and press materials.

## Message Rules

- Keep every slide simple and benefit-first.
- Do not mention APIs, keys, Cloudflare, WebXR, ARKit, ARCore, or implementation details.
- Position Philly Tours as a native app for real AR projection, with the webapp as a companion preview.
- Lead with history, exploration, compass logic, narration, and the native app handoff.
