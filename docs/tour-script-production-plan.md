## Writing Standard For Final Scripts

Each stop should do at least three things:

1. name the place clearly
2. explain why it matters in the route arc
3. sound spoken, not essay-like

Good final scripts should:

- sound premium when heard aloud
- avoid repeating the same sentence rhythm on every stop
- include place texture, not just abstract history
- distinguish `drive` from `walk` instead of duplicating tone

## Best Launch Order

1. rewrite the `highest` tier fully
2. strengthen the `high` tier
3. do a light spoken-audio polish on the `medium` and `low` tiers
