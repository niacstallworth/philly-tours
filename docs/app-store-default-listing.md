## Description

```text
Philly Tours turns Philadelphia history into a guided city experience.

Choose a themed route, follow the Founders Compass, hear narrated stories, and track your discoveries on the Founders Board. Each tour is designed to help the city make sense as you move through it: what happened here, why it matters, and where the story goes next.

The native app gives you the full experience:

- Story-led Philadelphia tour routes
- Compass guidance toward the next stop
- Narration and stop context
- Weather and tour-day context
- Founders Board progress, streaks, and rewards
- Native AR moments at supported stops
- Companion handoff from the web preview

The web companion lets you preview tours before you go. The native iPhone and iPad app is the best path for touring, narration, compass guidance, and AR.

History should feel alive, clear, and easy to follow. Philly Tours is built to make learning feel like moving through a story.
```

## App Review Information

- Demo account required: `No`
- Basic app flow login required: `No`

Review notes:

```text
Philly Tours is a guided Philadelphia touring app with route previews, narration, compass guidance, Board progress, and native AR moments.

To test the main flow:
1. Open the app.
2. Create a local profile with any name and email address.
3. Open Home and choose a tour.
4. Use the Compass tab to view the next stop and route context.
5. Open the AR tab to review companion and AR handoff behavior.
6. Open Board to view progress and collection features.

Location permission is used while the app is open to show nearby stops, route progress, compass guidance, and discovery progress. Camera permission is used for native AR placement. Bluetooth permission can route narration to headphones or supported smart glasses.

If the reviewer is not physically in Philadelphia, the route previews, narration context, Compass screen, Board, Settings, and AR handoff screens remain available. Location-triggered stop claims may require being near the relevant Philadelphia stop.
```
