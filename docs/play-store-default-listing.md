## Full Description

```text
Philly Tours makes Philadelphia history easier to explore.

Pick a themed route, follow the Founders Compass, hear narrated stories, and build your Founders Board as you visit stops across the city. The app helps you understand the route, the story, and the next place to go without turning history into homework.

Features include:

- Guided Philadelphia history routes
- Compass-led stop order
- Narrated stories and route context
- Founders Board progress and rewards
- Native AR moments at supported stops
- Weather and tour-day context
- Web companion preview before you download

The web companion is useful for previewing routes. The native Android app is the full touring experience, including compass guidance, narration, route progress, and AR handoff.

Philly Tours is built for people who want history to feel alive while they move through the city.
```
